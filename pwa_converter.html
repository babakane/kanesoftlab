How it works

Our converter uses a WASM-powered esbuild engine (simulated for browser-only mode) to bundle your TSX/JSX, CSS, and assets into a single data-URI powered HTML file. It automatically embeds a Service Worker and manifest for PWA capabilities.

Looking for CLI mode?

For large production pipelines, use our kane-script Python utility. It's faster and handles recursive node_module exclusions natively.
