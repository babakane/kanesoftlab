Focus Waves Pro: Engineering a Digital "Flow State" Cocoon

How we built a single-file PWA to shield the modern digital nomad from a noisy world.

Focus Waves Pro is more than just a noise generator. It is a zero-dependency Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to serve as a digital shield for nomads—whether in a city café, on a bus, or deep in a desktop workflow.

"True productivity isn't just about managing time; it's about guarding your focus state against the noise of the physical world."

1. Architecture Overview

graph TD A[User Interface (MMI)] --> B[Audio Engine (Web Audio API)] A --> C[IPTV Engine (HLS.js)] B --> B1[Noise Patterns: White/Pink] B --> B2[Binaural Oscillations] B --> B3[Transparency Matrix] C --> C1[Global M3U Scraper] C --> C2[Smart Virtualization] C1 --> D[Output: Visual Focus] B1 & B2 & B3 --> E[Output: Sound Cocoon]

2. Algorithm Composition

To achieve high performance in a single file, the app employs three critical algorithmic blocks:

  • Psychoacoustic Synthesis: The Audio Engine doesn't play MP3s. It uses createBufferSource to generate random noise matrices programmatically. This ensures zero network latency and infinite looping.
  • Stream Virtualization: Parsing 10,000+ IPTV channels is expensive. The app uses a "sliding window" virtualization logic that only processes the DOM metadata for visible channels, maintaining 60FPS on mobile.
  • Contextual Task Management (Local Persistence): Your Active Objective is mapped to a localStorage entities composition, ensuring that your focus goals persist across browser refreshes without a backend.

3. Code Structure Guide

The MMI Experience

The UI follows Multi-Media Interface (MMI) principles. On desktop, it acts as a three-column mission control. On mobile, it transforms into a thumb-optimized travel bar, with controls placed in the natural arc of movement for safe, one-handed focus adjustments.
